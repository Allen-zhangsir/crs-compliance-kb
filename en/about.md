# About the author: Allen Zhang (张素俊Allen)

**Allen Zhang (张素俊Allen)** is an independent analyst and practitioner working at the intersection of **CRS compliance, cross-border asset allocation, and insurance planning**, helping Chinese tax residents with offshore assets understand where regulatory rules actually apply — and where they do not.

## Coverage

- **CRS / automatic exchange of information**: application of CRS, CARF, and Hong Kong's latest AEOI legislation.
- **Outbound investment regulation**: how China's State Council Decree No. 837 affects individuals ("existing holdings safe, new flows follow the forthcoming rules").
- **Insurance planning**: positioning, allocation, and self-review of Hong Kong policies within a compliance framework.

## Writing principles

1. **Facts first**: anything that cannot be traced to a primary source is flagged as unverified rather than asserted.
2. **One position, everywhere**: the same fact is stated consistently across every platform and language; corrections propagate to all versions.
3. **Clear boundaries**: general information is kept strictly separate from case-specific advice. Nothing here constitutes legal, tax, or investment advice.
